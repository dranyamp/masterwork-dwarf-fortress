Clothing

Quite normal clothing for everyone. Warlocks can enchant robes with special effects, which only affect Warlocks.

Skeletons won't mind wearing no clothes, but they will still put them on if any are available. Ghouls actually do get a tiny bit sad about having none. Warlocks certainly want clothes.

           

Illumination

Illuminations are made from rock in the Craftsman's Shop, or Bonecarver, Stonecutter and Gemcutter Shops; from metal in the forge.

     
     
     

Instruments

Warlocks don't use musical instruments, but rather instruments of torture.

Instruments are made from rock in the Craftsman's Shop, or Bonecarver, Stonecutter and Gemcutter Shops; from wood in the Woodcrafter Shop; from metal in the forge.

     

Tools

Nestboxes and Hives are unknown to Warlocks. They don't need weak animals.

Tools are made from rock in the Craftsman's Shop, or Bonecarver, Stonecutter and Gemcutter Shops; from wood in the Woodcrafter Shop; from metal in the forge.
