Warlocks introduce only a few new materials. Ethereal (stone), Ethereal (metal), Empyrean (metal) and Dreadnought (metal). They do use some special materials that are known from Dwarf Mode, so I will make it short.

Ironbone is an iron-grade metal from the Corrupted Forge. Its made of ash and bone.

Bloodsteel is a steel-grade metal from the Corrupted Forge. Its made of ironbone and blood.

Dreadnought is a metal slightly superior to steel, but heavier. It gives 50% extra damage reduction to units that wear armor made of it. Its made of bloodsteel and souls.

Color-Name Ethereal can be any color. Its a free block or furniture material from the Ethereal Gate that allows building rooms and walls quickly.

Ethereal metal is an iron-grade metal from the Ethereal Forge. It vaporizes after 3-4 months, but its free. You can summon unlimited equpiment that way, but you wont keep it for long.

Empyrean metal is a steel-grade metal from the Ethereal Forge. It vaporizes after 1 month, but its free. Again, its unlimited, but will disappear after a while.

Netherbark is a steel-grade, constantly cold leather. You get it from your Herbalist, made from nethercap logs. They only grow in the third cavern.
